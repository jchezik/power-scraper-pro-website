Acceptable Use Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Power Scraper Pro is designed to help you organize and manage your personal media library. This policy outlines acceptable and prohibited uses of our software.

Intended Uses

Power Scraper Pro is intended for:

  • Organizing your personal movie and TV collection (DVDs, Blu-rays, physical media you own)
  • Organizing recordings from your personal DVR or streaming subscriptions
  • Organizing files downloaded from legitimate sources (iTunes, Amazon, Google Play, etc.)
  • Organizing your home videos and personal recordings

Prohibited Uses

Power Scraper Pro is NOT intended for and must NOT be used with:
