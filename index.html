Features

Everything you need to organize your media

Built from the ground up for macOS, Power Scraper Pro combines intelligent automation with professional-grade features to transform your media library.

Intelligent Scanning

Analyzes your media folders with advanced filename detection, accurately interpreting even complex movie and TV file names to automatically extract titles, release years, and quality details.

Multi-Source Metadata

Pulls rich, accurate metadata from TMDB and Fanart.TV, including ratings, cast, crew, summaries, and a wide selection of artwork for every title.

Kodi-Compliant Output

Automatically sorts loose media files into properly named folders, generates compliant NFO files, and arranges your library in the precise directory layout Kodi requires — ready to use right out of the box.

Apple Silicon Optimized

Designed natively for Apple Silicon with GPU-accelerated image processing via Metal. Posters and thumbnails render instantly, and scrolling stays buttery smooth even with 500+ movies.

NAS-Optimized Scanning

Delivers dramatically faster performance on network-attached storage with 10–20× speed boosts and a streamlined single-pass scanner that eliminates traditional file-by-file overhead.

Resume From Quit

Handles large scraping jobs effortlessly by remembering your exact progress, allowing the app to resume instantly after a quit or crash — no rescanning needed.

Smart Watch Folders

Automatically monitors designated movie and TV folders for new content. Choose a custom output destination, and Power Scraper Pro will process and relocate files while keeping them visible in your in-app library.

Drag & Drop Folder Support

Quickly add media to your library by dragging folders directly onto the app. Power Scraper Pro will immediately scan and process all media files inside.

Organize your collection

TV Shows

Every episode, perfectly organized

Intelligent season detection and episode tracking keeps your TV library in perfect order.

  • Detects season and episode numbers
  • Fetches show, season, and episode data
  • Tracks season completion percentage
  • Identifies missing episodes
  • Creates tvshow.nfo and episode.nfo
  • Downloads season-specific artwork
  • Supports specials (Season 0)
