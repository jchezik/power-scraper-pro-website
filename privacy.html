Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

Power Scraper Pro ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our macOS application.

Information We Do Not Collect

Power Scraper Pro is designed with privacy as a priority. We want to be clear about what we do not do:

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not track your usage or behavior
  • We do not store your media library information on our servers
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not use analytics or tracking tools

Local Data Storage

All data processed by Power Scraper Pro remains on your local machine:

  • Media files: Your movie and TV show files are processed locally and never uploaded anywhere
  • Metadata: Downloaded metadata and artwork are stored locally in your library folders
  • Preferences: App settings are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS preferences
  • Database: The app's SQLite database is stored locally and contains only information about your media library

Third-Party Services

Power Scraper Pro connects to the following third-party services:

  • The Movie Database (TMDB): Used to retrieve movie and TV show metadata, ratings, and artwork. Your API requests are made directly to TMDB's servers. Please review TMDB's Privacy Policy.
  • TheTVDB (TVDB): Used to retrieve TV show metadata and episode information. Please review TVDB's Privacy Policy.
  • Fanart.TV: Used to retrieve additional high-resolution artwork. Please review Fanart.TV's Privacy Policy.
  • Paddle: Our payment processor handles all purchases, billing, and license management. When you purchase Power Scraper Pro, Paddle collects payment information, email address, and billing details as the Merchant of Record. Please review Paddle's Privacy Policy.

When using metadata services, only the title information necessary to find matches is sent. No personal information is transmitted to metadata providers.

API Keys

You provide your own API keys for TMDB and Fanart.TV. These keys are stored locally on your Mac and are only used to authenticate your requests to these services. We never have access to your API keys.

Network Requests

The app makes network requests only for:

  • Fetching metadata from TMDB and Fanart.TV
  • Downloading poster and fanart images
  • Checking for app updates (optional)

Data Retention

Since Power Scraper Pro does not collect personal data, there is no data retention policy for user data on our end. However:

  • Local Data: Your media library data, preferences, and database remain on your Mac until you delete them
  • Payment Data: Paddle retains your purchase and billing information according to their privacy policy and legal requirements
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, your settings are synced via Apple's iCloud and subject to Apple's privacy policy

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data:

European Union (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access any personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing of your data
  • Data portability

Since we do not collect personal data through the app, these rights primarily apply to purchase data held by Paddle. Contact Paddle directly for purchase-related data requests.

California (CCPA/CPRA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Say no to the sale of personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Equal service and price (non-discrimination)

We do not sell personal information. For purchase-related data, contact Paddle.

Exercising Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at powerscraperpro@gmail.com. For purchase-related data held by Paddle, please contact Paddle directly through your order receipt.

Children's Privacy

Power Scraper Pro does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13 years of age (or 16 in the EU).

International Data Transfers

Power Scraper Pro is based in the United States. If you access the website or purchase from outside the US:

  • Payment processing through Paddle may involve international data transfers
  • Metadata requests go directly to TMDB, TVDB, and Fanart.TV servers
  • By using the software, you consent to any such transfer of information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at powerscraperpro@gmail.com.
