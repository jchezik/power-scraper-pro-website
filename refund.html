Refund Policy

Last updated: January 3, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with Power Scraper Pro. If you're not happy with your purchase for any reason, we offer a straightforward 14-day money-back guarantee.

14-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 14-day, no-questions-asked refund policy on all purchases of Power Scraper Pro. If you're not satisfied with the software for any reason within 14 days of your purchase, simply contact us for a full refund.

  • Refunds are available within 14 days of your original purchase date
  • No questions asked - you don't need to provide a reason

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, you can:

  • Email us: Send a refund request to powerscraperpro@gmail.com with your order number or the email address used for purchase
  • Use Paddle: Contact Paddle directly through the receipt email you received at purchase

We aim to process all refund requests within 2-3 business days. Once approved, the refund will be credited to your original payment method. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.

After Your Refund

Once a refund is processed:

  • Your license key will be deactivated
  • You will no longer have access to software updates
  • Any media files you organized with Power Scraper Pro will remain in their organized state - we don't undo your work

Exceptions

While we offer a generous refund policy, please note:

  • Refund requests made after 14 days from purchase are not eligible
  • Refunds are limited to one per customer - if you've previously received a refund for Power Scraper Pro, subsequent purchases are not eligible
  • Chargebacks or disputes filed with your bank instead of contacting us directly may result in your license being permanently revoked

EU Consumer Rights

If you are a consumer in the European Union, you have the right to withdraw from a purchase within 14 days without giving any reason, as provided by the EU Consumer Rights Directive. Our 14-day money-back guarantee aligns with and exceeds these requirements.
